691 <div class="ct-body3 tr-indent2"><p style="margin-top:1ex; margin-bottom:1ex;">Phase 1: Oral SNDX-5613; sequential cohorts of escalating dose levels of SNDX-5613 to identify the MTD and RP2D. Participants will be enrolled in one of six dose-escalation arms:</p>

694 <p style="margin-top:0ex; margin-bottom:1ex;">Arm C: Participants receiving SNDX-5613 and cobicistat.</p>

695 <p style="margin-top:0ex; margin-bottom:1ex;">Arm D: Participants receiving fluconazole (moderate CYP3A4 inhibitor) for antifungal prophylaxis.</p>

696 <p style="margin-top:0ex; margin-bottom:1ex;">Arm E: Participants not receiving any weak, moderate, or strong CYP3A4 inhibitors/inducers.</p>

697 <p style="margin-top:0ex; margin-bottom:1ex;">Arm F: Participants receiving isavuconazole (moderate CYP3A4 inhibitor) for antifungal prophylaxis.</p>

698 <p style="margin-top:0ex; margin-bottom:1ex;">In Phase 2, participants will be enrolled in 3 indication-specific expansion cohorts to determine the efficacy, short- and long-term safety, and tolerability of SNDX-5613:</p>

700 <li style="margin-top:0.7ex;">Cohort 2A: Participants with MLLr acute lymphoblastic leukemia (ALL)/mixed phenotype acute leukemia (MPAL)</li>
