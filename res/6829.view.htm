680 <div class="ct-body3 tr-indent2"><p style="margin-top:1ex; margin-bottom:1ex;">This study is in patients aged &#8805;18 years old undergoing or having relapsed after their first allogeneic HSCT (matched sibling, matched unrelated donor, or haploidentical transplants) for AML.</p>

681 <p style="margin-top:0ex; margin-bottom:1ex;">Potential patients for the study may be screened/enrolled:</p>

682 <p style="margin-top:0ex; margin-bottom:1ex;">&#8226; Prior to their first allogeneic HSCT.</p>

683 <p style="margin-top:0ex; margin-bottom:1ex;">or</p>

684 <p style="margin-top:0ex; margin-bottom:1ex;">&#8226; Patients experiencing their first relapse post-allogeneic transplant.</p>

685 <p style="margin-top:0ex; margin-bottom:1ex;">Patients eligible for the study will be placed into one of two groups:</p>

686 <ul style="margin-top:1ex; margin-bottom:1ex;">

687 <li style="margin-top:0.7ex;">

688 <p style="margin-top:0ex; margin-bottom:1ex;">Adjuvant (Group 1): Patients screened prior to their HSCT with CR without minimal residual disease (CRMRD-) at 85-130 days post transplant will be randomized (1:1) in an unblinded fashion to:</p>

689 <ul style="margin-top:1ex; margin-bottom:1ex;">

690 <li style="margin-top:0.7ex;">MT-401 (Arm A)</li>

691 <li style="margin-top:0.7ex;">SOC (Arm B)</li>

692 </ul>

693 </li>

694 <li style="margin-top:0.7ex;">

695 <p style="margin-top:0ex; margin-bottom:1ex;">Active Disease: (Group 2): Patients meeting the following criteria will be assigned to Group 2 and will receive MT 401:</p>

696 <ul style="margin-top:1ex; margin-bottom:1ex;">

697 <li style="margin-top:0.7ex;">Patients who experience relapse (patients with MRD [MRD+] or frank relapse) at or prior to post-transplant Day 85-130</li>

698 <li style="margin-top:0.7ex;">Patients in Arm B of Group 1 (SOC) who develop relapse (MRD+ or frank relapse) post-HSCT (crossover patients)</li>

699 <li style="margin-top:0.7ex;">Patients who do not consent prior to HSCT but are experiencing their first relapse (MRD+ or frank relapse) and have the same donor available for manufacturing</li>
