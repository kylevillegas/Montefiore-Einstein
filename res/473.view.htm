509<h1>Quality in Life Team</h1>

510<h3>A community of caring to support your family</h3>

511<p>In times of uncertainty you are not alone. When your child is facing a life-threatening or life-limiting illness, we are here to support you and your family&rsquo;s physical, spiritual and emotional needs. We meet with a physician-led team to help you when:</p>

512<ul>

513<li>your child is facing an illness that threatens his or her life</li>

514<li>your child is in the hospital on an increasing basis</li>

515<li>it is becoming harder to care for your child at home</li>

516<li>treatments are not working or seem to be hurting more than helping</li>

517<li>you are feeling overwhelmed or are having trouble coping</li>

518</ul>

519<h3>The Quality in Life Team provides:</h3>

520<ul>

521<li>Advanced pain and symptom management to relieve suffering caused by pain and other problems that arise along your child&rsquo;s care journey</li>

522<li>Assistance with complex medical decision making, consistent with your values and goals</li>

523<li>Honest, frank communication and support whether your child is in the hospital, clinic or at home</li>

524<li>Hospice evaluation and referral</li>

525<li>Assists with inpatient care of a life limiting illness</li>

526<li>Works to help your child and family live life to the fullest each day</li>

527<li>Support to ease your emotional, social and spiritual concerns</li>

528<li>Strategies for stress reduction and pain management</li>

529</ul>

530<p><strong>When can the Quality in Life Team get involved?</strong><br />From the moment your child is diagnosed with a condition that threatens or limits their life. We work alongside your primary medical team throughout your child&rsquo;s care.</p>

531<p><strong>Who besides my child can benefit from the Quality in Life Team getting involved?</strong><br />When a member of your family is sick, it affects the whole family and the community of people that support you. We will work with you, your child, and your child&rsquo;s brothers and sisters. We can even work with your child&rsquo;s school.</p>

532<p><strong>How can my child meet the Quality in Life Team?</strong><br />Just ask for us! Find out more from your child&rsquo;s doctor, nurse, social worker or child life specialist.</p>

533<h3>The Quality in Life Team</h3>

534<p><em>Physician</em><br />Advanced pain and symptom management, goals of care clarification and assisting families with complex medical decisions</p>

535<p><em>Chaplain</em><br />Providing spiritual guidance for all faiths and coordinating bereavement support</p>

536<p><em>Psychologist</em><br />Counseling of families and children, also testing during cancer treatment and support groups</p>

537<p><em>Yoga Therapist</em><br />Stress and relaxation techniques for children, families and staff to ease suffering</p>

538<p><em>Social workers</em><br />Assists with care coordination, provides a valuable link to school and hosts support groups</p>
